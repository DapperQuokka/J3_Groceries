FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I have to create an account to shop?

Nope! You do not need to create an account to shop at J3 Groceries. In fact, we don't even have an account system in place! You simply add products to your cart and enter your address and payment information at checkout! It's as simple as that!

What is the minimum order?

$0.01! We have no minimum order amount! Buy however much you want of whatever you like!

How much does delivery cost?

We charge a $9.99 delivery fee in order to get your products to you in their freshest state possible.

Do you deliver to my area?

Yes! We deliver everywhere!

When do you deliver?

We send out your order next business day, at the latest. Delivery usually takes 2 to 3 business days.

How do you pack my groceries?

We pack them in a cardboard box filled with protective air bags to keep your groceries safe.

How can I pay for my order?

We currently only accept payments through PayPal.

How do I find specific items?

To find specific items, you can either use the search bar at the top of all of our pages, or browse by category or manufacturer using the browse bar at the top.

Can I suggest an item?

Yes! Click here to suggest an item.
